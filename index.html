The following functions are available in the console:

  • forEach(array, callback)
  • map(array, callback)
  • some(array, callback)
  • find(array, callback)
  • findIndex(array, callback)
  • every(array, callback)
  • filter(array, callback)
  • concat(array1, array2)
  • includes(array, value)
  • join(array, joiningString)
  • slice(array, startingIndex, endingIndex)

Their functionality mirrors that of the corresponding Array.prototype methods.
